Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we can arrange for invoicing.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for students with a valid .edu email address. Contact our support team to apply for the discount.

How do mock interviews work?

Mock interviews are 1-on-1 video sessions with experienced interviewers from top tech companies. You'll receive detailed feedback after each session to help you improve.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade at any time.

Can I switch plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.
